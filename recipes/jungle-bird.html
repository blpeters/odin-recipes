Jungle Bird

An incredible rum cocktail you won't find at an average tiki bar

Description

The jungle bird is a rum cocktail that isn't overly sweet with some deep flavors.

We will be using Pusser's Rum, which is the classic, dark rum used in tiki cocktails. However, feel free to experiment with white rum or even a different liquor entirely!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Pusser's Rum
  • .75 oz Campari
  • 1.5 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 0.5 oz lime juice
  • 0.5 oz Simple Syrup
  • Pineapple Wedge for Garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice
  2. Shake for at least 15 seconds.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass with ice.
  4. Serve with wedge of pineapple.
