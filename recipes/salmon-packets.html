Salmon Packets

Description

No pots or pans required for this one, and all ingredients go in at once. Bake for 15 minutes and your done. Just throw away the foil packets afterwards and cleanup is done too!

This recipe is enough to serve 2 meals.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 large sheets aluminum foil
  • 2 salmon filets
  • 1 white or yellow onion
  • 3 large carrots
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 385 degrees.
  2. arrange 2 sheets of aluminum foil side by side on a baking pan. Double up with 2 sheets each for added strength against tearing.
  3. Using a mandoline or knife, slice onion into ring and julienne cut the carrots.
  4. Divide vegetables between the two foil packets. Place a salmon filet on top in each packet.
  5. Add salt, rice vinegar, sesame oil and grated fresh ginger to the tops of the salmon. Divide equally between packets
  6. Carefully close packets, crimping the edges while leaving air space at the top for heat circulation.
  7. Bake at 385 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
